what is a math circle?

A Welcoming Space to Explore Challenging and Creative Math Together

In Math Circles, students meet with skilled teachers to tackle engaging problems, think independently, and connect math to the world around them.

Why math circles?

Building Skills, Confidence, and Community Through Math

Deepen Mathematical Understanding and Enjoyment

Explore challenging concepts that go beyond the classroom, sparking curiosity and a lasting love for math.

Develop Critical Thinking and Personal Growth

Tackle complex problems, build resilience, and grow confidence in your ability to think independently.

Promote Collaboration and Future Readiness

Work with peers in a supportive community while gaining skills that prepare you for college, careers, and lifelong learning

our programs

Connecting Passion for Math with Opportunity for All

Math Circles of Chicago offers engaging programs that spark curiosity and open doors for the future. Powered by passionate students, supportive families, and a network of educators and community partners, we make high-quality math enrichment accessible to all Chicago students.

Math Circles
Grades 3–8
During Academic Year

Bringing students and teachers together through HUBS and After School Programs to explore challenging, creative math.

College Pathways
Grades 9–12
Fall, Winter & Summer

Empowers high school students to explore advanced math, STEM careers, and their own potential.

QED

December, 2025

A Math Symposium where students showcase original projects, from puzzles to real-world problem solving.

Math Festivals
All Ages
Year-round

Fun, hands-on events where students and families explore math through games and puzzles.

Summer Camps
Students in Rising 6–12th Grade
Summers

Two weeks of collaborative, creative math in a fun, community-focused setting, often outdoors to enjoy the beautiful summer weather.
